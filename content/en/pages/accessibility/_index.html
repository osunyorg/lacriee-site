Website

La Criée centre d’art contemporain website benefits from Osuny's excellent accessibility quality and adheres as much as possible to the "Web Content Accessibility Guidelines" and the "Référentiel Général d'Amélioration de l'Accessibilité". 

La Criée

People with reduced mobility:

-- There are two reserved parking spaces in front of La Criée.

Pictogram indicating parking accessibility for people with motor disabilities

-- La Criée is accessible by wheelchair and with the Handistar service.

Pictogram indicating accessibility for people with motor disabilities

-- You can request a cane or seats at the reception.

Pictogram indicating accessibility for people with reduced mobility

For visually impaired visitors:

-- You can request a large print visitor guide at the reception.

Pictogram indicating accessibility for blind or visually impaired people

For deaf and hard of hearing visitors:

-- A descriptive and tactile tour is offered for each exhibition.

Pictogram indicating accessibility for people with hearing impairment or disability

-- Tours are translated into French Sign Language.

in partnership with AÑVOL

Pictogram indicating accessibility for people with hearing impairment or disability

For groups with mental or psychological disabilities:

-- Tours are accompanied by materials that facilitate understanding of the artworks

Pictogram indicating accessibility for people with mental or psychological disabilities
