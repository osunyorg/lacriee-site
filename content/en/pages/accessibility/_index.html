La Criée centre d’art contemporain website benefits from Osuny's excellent accessibility quality and adheres as much as possible to the "Web Content Accessibility Guidelines - external link" and the "Référentiel Général d'Amélioration de l'Accessibilité - external link".

People with reduced mobility: -- There are two reserved parking spaces in front of La Criée.

-- La Criée is accessible by wheelchair and with the Handistar service.

For visually impaired visitors: -- You can request a large print visitor guide at the reception.

image: id: "15e3ae6b-f7d2-4750-9e50-76c3ec6107ad" file: "15e3ae6b-f7d2-4750-9e50-76c3ec6107ad" alt: >- Pictogram indicating accessibility for blind or visually impaired people credit: >- - title: >- description: >-

For deaf and hard of hearing visitors: -- A descriptive and tactile tour is offered for each exhibition.
