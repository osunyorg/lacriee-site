Since 1986, La Criée center d'art contemporain has been designing, producing, co-producing and presenting annual temporary exhibitions representative of contemporary art. These solo and group exhibitions are invitations to emerging and established artists working in Brittany, France and internationally. The art center also co-produces several off-site exhibitions each season, in Rennes, Brittany, France and abroad.
