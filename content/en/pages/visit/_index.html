Access

Address La Criée centre d'art contemporain Honoré Place Commeurec 35000 Rennes Coming by public transport metro line a : Republique metro line b : Colombier Bus 1, 5, 9 : La Criée Coming by bike Star bike station : Republique and Colombier parking around La Criée Handistar access and central hall parking The Criée is accessible to people with reduced mobility.

Come alone, with friends or family

The Criée makes available to you visiting document that presents the exhibition and works to support you in your visit. The host team is present to answer your questions and discuss the artists' approach. A documentation space offers a book selection
in consultation on exhibitions: monographics book of artists, catalogues, journal, etc. Publications published by La Criée are on sale in the shop space publications, multiple artists, poster, etc.

Visits, workshops and events

Find visits for all, workshops for children and family, events, meetings with artists, etc. on the event tab.
