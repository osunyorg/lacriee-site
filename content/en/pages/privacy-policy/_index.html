Personal Data

The personal data collected on the site results from voluntary communication when registering for La Criée's newsletter. Your personal data will not be used for marketing purposes, direct mailing, and will not be sold. At any time, you can contact us at la-criee@ville-rennes.fr to delete, modify, or request your personal data.

Copyright

All reproduction rights are reserved, including for downloadable documents, iconographic and photographic representations. The reproduction of all or part of this site on an electronic medium is strictly prohibited, except with the express authorization of the publication director.

Audience Measurement

The site uses Plausible , a European audience measurement system that complies with CNIL recommendations.
