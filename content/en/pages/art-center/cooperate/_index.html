At local and national level

Every year, La Criée contemporary art center develops many regular and one-off collaborations at the local and national levels. These partnerships bring together professionals from the fields of art and culture, as well as the education, social, and health sectors, reflecting a constant focus on collective work and regional roots. Among these long-term collaborations is the Territoires EXTRA program, designed in 2017 with the Passerelle art center in Brest, with the support of the DRAC Bretagne - Ministry of Culture. This program aims to support emerging artists through production and promotion, to develop projects in close collaboration with partners in Brittany, and to place these initiatives within an international context.

DCA professional meetings, Art centres in transition , November 25 and 26, 2024 at the Maison des Associations, Rennes -- Photo: Estelle Chaigne

In parallel with these collaborative projects, and drawing on the
expertise developed through its activities, La Criée team is actively
involved in the reflection and structuring of the cultural sector. It is
regularly invited to participate in committees and professional
meetings, and is involved in networks at various levels, contributing to
the sharing of knowledge, experience, and professional practices. It is
a member of the following networks: 

DCA - Association française de développement des centres d'art contemporain
https://dca-art.com/

BLA! - Association nationale des professionnel·le·s de la médiation en art contemporain
https://blamediation.fr/

a.c.b – art contemporain en Bretagne
https://www.artcontemporainbretagne.org/

At international level

La Criée is committed to the production and promotion of international projects through artist and curator residencies, co-produced artworks and exhibitions, co-publications, and research programmes. By collaborating with a wide range of partners worldwide, the art centre supports the international circulation of the artists' projects it accompanies.
