La Criée supports artists in the production of their works. This includes financial, artistic, logistical and technical support.

- kind: heading title: >- Programming exhibitions slug: "programming-exhibitions" position: 2 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

La Criée produces three temporary solo or group exhibitions a year. The space, which is transformed according to artistic projects, presents works - most often produced for the occasion - by emerging and established artists working in Brittany, France and internationally.
It also co-produces several off-site exhibitions each season, in Rennes, Brittany, France and abroad.

- kind: heading title: >- Programming events slug: "programming-events" position: 3 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

All exhibitions are combined with multidisciplinary events. Performances, concerts, lectures, screenings and collective readings punctuate the program, inviting us to shift the way we discover the work of guest artists.

- kind: heading title: >- Hosting artists in residence slug: "hosting-artists-in-residence" position: 4 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

La Criée develops research, creation and transmission residencies in line with the needs of artists, in partnership with cultural, educational and health facilities, etc.

- kind: heading title: >- Associating artists slug: "associating-artists" position: 5 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

La Criée has developed a unique format for an art center, based on a model inspired by live entertainment. Each season, La Criée associates one or more artists with its programming. This collaborative format allows us to experiment with a new way of working over the long term, and to be as close as possible to the creative process and the movements of thought.
