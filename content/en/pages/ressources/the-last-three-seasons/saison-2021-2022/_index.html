The 2021-2022 season welcomed 30,132 visitors (including 2583 outside the walls), 5 exhibitions (including 2 outside the walls), 15 events, 22 works produced or co-produced, 4 residences (3 men, 1 woman), 54 artists supported (including 30 women and 24 men).

This season is part of the cycle Lili, la rozell et le marimba: vernaculaire et création contemporaine which associated the artists and exhibition curators Lotte Arndt, Jean-Roch Bouille, Baptiste Brun, John Cornu, Katia Kameli and Émilie Renard.
