La Criée's space, reception conditions and tools are renewed for each exhibition. These include object books, tactile mats, games of shape, color, light and shadow, chosen or designed in dialogue with the works on show.
To find out more, please contact the mediator in charge of young visitors.

The Mini-pousse visit is a way of awakening the senses and discovering the works of art.

It's aimed at toddlers and their carers: nursery nurses, early childhood educators, childminders, parent assistants, childcare workers or ATSEMs.
