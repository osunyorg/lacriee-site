Accompanying persons are invited to contact the mediation team to specify visit arrangements. These vary according to exhibitions and can be adapted to group specificities.

Due to space constraints, La Criée can only accommodate one group at a time in its exhibition space. Therefore, it is recommended to reserve your time slot.

Guided Visit

Accompanied by a cultural mediator, visits are privileged moments to meet, exchange, and express. It can possibly include small workshop like activities or games.

notes: >-

Group reception by request and reservation:
Tuesday to Friday, from 9:30 AM to 6:00 PM
Duration: 1 hour, Free for groups of 4 or more people

Self-Guided Visit

For self-guided visits led by the accompanying person, La Criée provides at reception: Easy-to-read-and-understand visit booklets, game booklets, or observation tools. For small groups, reception staff are available to answer questions about the exhibition.

notes: >-

Open Tuesday to Saturday, from 1:00 PM to 6:00 PM
Reservation recommended Maximum group of 10 people
Duration: 30 minutes

These courses are intended for young people in professional integration, users of social centers, CCAS, CDAS, people in residential homes, child in social aid, youth in judicial protection, beneficiaries of national solidarity associations, etc.

Defined with liaison persons from these structures, paths can include meetings with artist games in the host structure, exhibition visits at La Criée and other Rennes cultural venues, thematic workshops crossed with other artistic fields, presentations, etc.

notes: >-

Duration: 4 half-days or over a week during school holidays.

These paths are developed primarily with professionals who have completed the « Passeurs de Culture » professional training.

The art center co-constructs actions combining arts discovery, exhibition practice, and exercise of cultural rights, in a dynamic of inclusion, exchange, and sharing. Based on its artistic programming and in partnership with host structures, La Criée supports artists in their research projects or shared creation with residents. This can be in priority neighborhoods of Rennes, in penitentiary centers, or in rural areas.

notes: >-

Projects are defined one year in advance, in partnership and with support from local authorities and the Ministry of Culture / Drac Bretagne, within the framework of Territoires EXTRA programs (carried by art centers La Criée, in Rennes, and Passerelle, in Brest), Culture & Justice, Culture and City Policy, or Culture and Solidarities.
