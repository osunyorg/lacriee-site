Before your visit, our cultural mediators invite you to a discussion at La Criée to discover the exhibition, the tools we offer, and define visit arrangements together, considering your educational project.

Resources are also accessible from our website: artist games or workshop ideas in the educational resources section, to extend the visit at the leisure center.

notes: >-

By request and appointment

Più o meno Visits : After-school Workshops

During lunch breaks or after school, two options are offered:
-- A Più visit, to engage hands and ideas during a workshop
-- A Meno visit, to encounter an artwork at one's own pace

notes: >-

Tuesday to Friday, from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM and from 4:00 PM
Duration: 45 minutes

Visite à modeler : Leisure Centers

The Visit à modeler favors a playful or sensory approach to artworks, taking a step aside to observe and express creativity. And sometimes, there are surprises to taste or take away!

notes: >-

Wednesday afternoons and during school holidays, Tuesday to Friday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM
Duration: 1h30. Group of 15 maximum

These paths are co-constructed with animation teams from Rennes from metropolitan area's leisure centers around a thematic entry. They include exhibition visits combined with artistic practice sessions, at La Criée and at the leisure center, in partnership with another cultural venue.

These courses can, for example, combine visual arts and performing arts, according to the artistic programming of Rennes cultural venues.

notes: >-

Duration: over a day or a week, during school holidays
