For Groups with Mental or Psychological Disabilities

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: features title: >- slug: >- ranks: base: 3 children: 3 top: active: false data: description: >- elements: - title: >- description: >-

-- Guided visit:

The cultural mediator offers a visit with simplified communication using visual aids to facilitate artwork discovery and understanding. The visit prioritizes exchanges and sharing.

Guided visits available by request and reservation Tuesday to Friday
From 2:00 PM to 7:00 PM
Duration: 30 min to 1 hour depending on exhibitions

-- Self-guided visit:

To help you freely discover exhibitions, "Easy to read and understand" (FALC) booklets are available at reception in the form of an exhibition visit guide and an art center presentation booklet.

For People with Reduced Mobility

-- Guided visit:

Cultural mediators offer a commented exhibition tour with presentation of the art center, artist, and exhibited works, focusing on exchanges and gentle mobility.

Guided visits available by request and reservation Tuesday to Friday
From 2:00 PM to 7:00 PM

Individually or in groups, you can freely use cane-seats available at La Criée's reception.

Since 2006, La Criée has been working with people in care at hospitals, medical-social, or pedagogical centers, at the invitation of health managers, teachers, or educators, in various ways. This may include artist video projections, writing or artistic practice workshops, thematic interventions with games or artist books, offered based on the art center's exhibitions. The public services department also supports creation projects within artist residencies in hospital settings, programmed in connection with La Criée's artistic cycles and the host structure's cultural project.

notes: >-

Projects are defined one year in advance and lead to signing an agreement with the health facility, in partnership and with support from the Ministry of Health – ARS and Ministry of Culture – DRAC Bretagne, within the Culture-Health program.
