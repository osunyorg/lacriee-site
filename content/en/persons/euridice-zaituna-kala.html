Euridice Zaituna Kala is a Mozambican artist-teacher. the work focuses on cultural and historical metamorphoses, its manipulations and adaptations. The artist reproduces the vocabulary visual of historical archives to reveal its subjectivities, but also those they have invisibility. It questions the appropriation of black bodies by their representation in archives; But rather than to seize their history, she tries to reaffirm their existence. His work takes the form of installations, performances, images, objects and books.
