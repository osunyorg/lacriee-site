Sophie Kaplan is the director of La Criée centre d'art contemporain Since 2012. His critical approach and his curatorial practice is develop around the importance attached to collaborations – in particular with artists via the setting up at La Criée des cycles and associated artists; from the space left to the as engines of research, creation and transmission; from interest to the crossing of the arts, disciplines and knowledge.
