Artist

body: >-


Born in 1969 in Montpellier, lives and works in Paris, France. He is represented by Meessen De Clercq Gallery, Brussels.


Nominated for the Marchel Duchamp Prize (2014), he exhibited at
Centre Pompidou (2011), the Palais de Tokyo and the Grand Palais (2013),
MUDAM Luxembourg (2015), MUCEM in Marseille (2018) and at the Louvre Museum
Lens (2021). Evariste Richer is the author of a poetic work
who presents himself as an exploration of reality and strives to understand
the universe and mechanisms that have generated and continue to enliven it.
By seizing the tools of science and culture such as
meteorology, teleology, astronomy, physics, it sets up
devices that help provide a new reading grid without
stalemate on an intrinsic aesthetic dimension of which
The aim is to reconcile the individual with the universal.
The minimalist and conceptual aesthetic that governs the creations of
The artist disturbed by his power of suggestion and convocation
and built around the spectator a narrative that questions our systems of
thinking and shaking our understanding of the world. (source: Untilthen Gallery)
