Artist, forestry and landscaper

body: >-


Born in 1987 in Strasbourg, lives in Bourg-des-Comptes and works in Saint-Jacques-de-la-Lande.


Lea Muller takes another look at the surrounding territory and
develops tools for appropriating and reading the landscape. She
We are convinced to forge a position on how we shape our
territories: develop less and understand better, build with a
High concern for what pre-existing and resources,
sensitivity to ordinary landscapes, paying attention to the living,
design the landscape as the concrete and visible materialization of
our report in the world. For the sake of anchoring,
a concrete transformation of a company model, Lea Muller develops a
project of soft forestry and direct processing on its
forest plots in Bourg‐des-Comptes (35).
