body: >-


40 copies118,9 x 175 cm


Coproduction La Criée, Musée des beaux-arts de Rennes, Frac Bretagne. This work was originally conceived for the Art Across the City program (a public art festival) and appeared in large format on the wall of a shopping center in Swansea, Wales. In a globalized world where commercial trade is destroying the planet, Deller advocates for a return to more poetry.
In Rennes, the poster was displayed on urban billboards throughout the city all summer.
