body: >-


59 x 83 cm, printed off-set on both sides 3000 copies


Poster designed in response to a commission from the London Underground. The bicycle features the colors and style of the tube map. Jeremy Deller offers these posters for free as part of the exhibition of his collection of printed works, Warning Graphic Content.
To prevent their resale, he has placed a curse on them. On the back of the poster, the following phrase is printed: "This poster is given away for free, but if you sell it, you will be cursed and die a horrible death."
