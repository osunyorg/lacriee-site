body: >-


LiZellBa also exists in a digital version, where players play individually. The principle of the game is similar to the board version: players
discover an empty exhibition space and gradually place artworks produced
by La Criée, obtained by answering questions about the exhibitions. Play online : lizellba.la-criee.org
