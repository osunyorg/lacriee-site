body: >-


40 copies118,9 x 175 cm


Coproduction La Criée, Musée des beaux-arts de Rennes, Frac Bretagne.
Jeremy Deller designed this poster for the Art Party Conference in 2013.
The title I've Got the Power refers to the song The Power by Snap!,
a German Eurodance group (1990). The object on the poster is a
prehistoric arrowhead. The poster was displayed on urban billboards
throughout the city all summer.
