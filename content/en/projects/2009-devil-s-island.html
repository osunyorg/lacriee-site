a hexagonal sculpture that refers to another form of
disciplinary power: Panoptic. Materialized as a
prison building of circular form, Panoptic allows
monitor inmates at every moment, without the inmates
may know if they are observed, creating a feeling
invisible omniscience. Designed by philosopher and lawyer Jeremy
Bentham in 1870, Panoptics will be critically studied by the
