body: >-


printings on tarpaulins: 220 x 450 cm, 170 x 185 cm, 225 x 270 cm, 185 x 170 cm


Alongside her residency, Mathilde Seguin created a temporary installation entitled Des vues : Côté rue (Views: Street Side). During June 2009, these windows printed on tarpaulins were hung as trompe-l'oeil on the facades of the school, the Villejean social center's childcare facility, and other buildings in Rennes. This installation gave rise to a series of visits with students and residents of Villejean, organized as part of Architecture Week in Brittany.
supports : -- ministère de la Culture / Drac Bretagne -- ville de Rennes partnerships : -- académie de Rennes / DSDEN35 -- école Kennedy, Rennes
procurement

: fonds communal d’art contemporain de la ville de Rennes
