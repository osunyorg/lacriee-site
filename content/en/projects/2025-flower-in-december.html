body: >-


Flower in December is a sculpture by Lucie Férézou made of aluminium, paraffin and rose petals. Its material is inspired by the effect of frozen herbs in the winter ice. Its shape reproduces the contours of a charcoal seed, symbol of metamorphosis. It can hang or hang on the wall and diffuses a pink magic around its environment.
