Remise en jeu (2) is a fictional exhibition designed by artist Clémence Estève and graphic designer Lohengrin Papadato, conceived as a possible variation of Remise en jeu . The photomontages offer a glimpse into the diversity of forms, texts, and images created by the students of Torigné Elementary School with artist Clémence Estève throughout her research, creation, and transmission residency. The productions were inspired by the discoveries of Gallo-Roman archaeological remains, meetings with archaeologists, and visits to the excavation site of the Hôtel-Dieu in Rennes, led by the National Institute for Preventive Archaeological Research (Inrap) in 2016-2017. On the front of the edition: -- Réinterprétations de relevés archéologiques by Louis Frédéric, topographic drawings and vues du chantier de fouilles of the Hôtel-Dieu, photographs, Clémence Estève, 2016-2017  -- Œuvres collectives , engravings, painting, pottery, and Abécédaire, CM1 class, Torigné school, 2017  -- Plan de Condate (Rennes in Antiquity) with the tracing of Gallo-Roman roads , Mathilde Dupré, Inrap, 2016 On the back of the edition:  -- Voir 1, Voir 2, Voir 3, Voir 4, Voir 5 , digital collages, Clémence Estève, 2017

production : La Criée centre d’art contemporain, Rennes Partners : -- Inrap -- école Torigné, Rennes -- Académie de Rennes / DSDEN 35 Supports : -- ministère de la Culture et de la Communication / Drac Bretagne -- direction éducation enfance de la ville de Rennes graphic design : Lohengrin Papadato
