Amadou Sanogo

body: >-


162 × 160 cm, acrylic on canvas





A white, bearded figure appears in a frame painted on a black background. This painting, created in Rennes, conveys a sense of uncertainty and the unknown. The proverb says that you can never know how things will turn out. In other words, you should not prejudge anything, and hope that things will not turn out as predicted.
