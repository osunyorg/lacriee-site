body: >-


performance, 15 min



Francis Raynaud's work, Objets de prestidigitation (Objects of Sleight of Hand), presented in the exhibition Sculpter (faire à l'atelier) (Sculpting (in the workshop)), is a performance ‘in hibernation’. Its activation by performers and conjurers is entitled Les six faces d'un chapeau (The Six Sides of a Hat) and takes place three times during the exhibition. Performing magic tricks is, in a way, like doing DIY and sculpture. This performance creates a link between art, ‘bricology’ and conjuring.
