megaphone and ammonite40 × 24 × 30 cm


Festina Lente consists of a megaphone whose amplifying horn is
obstructed by an ammonite of the same diameter. This fortuitous
correspondence represents for Evariste Richer 'a collision of chance.'
The work juxtaposes discourses of urgency with the silence of sediments
thousands of years old. The speech induced by the megaphone extends the
spiral of geological time embodied by the fossil.
