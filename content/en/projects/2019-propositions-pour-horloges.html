David Horvitz

body: >-

posters in French and Breton displayed across the city


Propositions pour horloges is a poetic alternative to the standard for measuring time. Composed in English, then translated into French and Breton, these visual poems are displayed at La Criée and scattered in the public space of Rennes to offer themselves surreptitiously to the passers-by. Time and space intersect to create a geopoetics, where the artist’s subjectivity blends with our imaginations.
courtesy of the artist, ChertLüdde, Berlin and Yvon Lambert Bookseller & Publisher
