Following exposure Devils Island held in La Criée from 22 May to 26 July 2009, La Criée centre d'art contemporain publishes the book Devils Island – Devil's Island dedicated to the production of Polish artist Hubert Czerepok.

This book is a real monograph all of Hubert Czerepok's artistic production. It details also works produced during the exhibition Devils Island. Important Polish theoreticians and curators of the exhibition written unpublished texts and interviews with the artist who troublematize a dense and complex work.
