David Horvitz

body: >-


unique projection of 19080 photos, 19080 min





The work is a slideshow of nineteen thousand eighty images from his personal archives. A computer program broadcasts each of them then permanently deletes them; we are then the only ones to see it at this moment and the only ones to see it disappear. The artist offers here to share his intimacy, while questioning us on the vanity of the overproduction of images in the digital age.




courtesy of the artist, ChertLüdde, Berlin and Yvon Lambert Bookseller & Publisher
