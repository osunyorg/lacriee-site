body: >-


Pollen is a screen printed drawing from pink ink, pollen powder, sand, mica, shell and glass. This work evokes a story without beginning or end as a fictional landscape ecosystem where the main characters are flowers, bees and a bird.

editor

La Criée centre d'art contemporain

technical characteristics

This multiple is printed in silkscreen from ink and pollen powder, sand, shell and glass
in collaboration with Boutefeu, Brest. It is

published in 100 copies in the residence La Multiplicité des étoiles (The multiplicity of stars) at the Jean Moulin School in Rennes, January to April 2025, associated with the
cycle Festina Lente (
Make haste slowly

).

price

10 €
