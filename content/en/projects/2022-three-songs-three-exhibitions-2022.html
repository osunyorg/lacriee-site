body: >-


three bouzouks, metal support, carpet, video, sound, three exhibitions


Three specific bouzoukis were commissioned from a luthier, each designed as a portrait of an exhibition created by the artist. On these bouzoukis, used only once, the luthier played a melody, a farewell theme for each past exhibition in Ghent and Beirut. These melodies will be played at La Criée at the sunset times of Ghent and Beirut.
-- Courtesy of the artist and Galeria Vera Cortês, Lisbon
