About

Nadine Atallah teaches art history and theory at the European School of
Fine Arts in Brittany (EESAB) in Rennes. Her research in art history is
linked to a curatorial practice focused on contemporary scenes from the
African continent and its diaspora, as well as gender issues. Together
with Euridice Zaituna Kala, who shares similar concerns, they will
jointly explore the exhibition at La Criée to provide some insights.
