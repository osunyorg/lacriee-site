Come and discover La Chuchoterie and its artists' books for toddlers and families! This free-play space is like a living exhibition, where anyone can pick up a book, discover it, handle it and share it. True artistic objects, La Chuchoterie's books invite you to dream, to imagine, and to spend an intimate moment of sharing, in a white and black cocoon made of cosy carpets and starry cushions.
