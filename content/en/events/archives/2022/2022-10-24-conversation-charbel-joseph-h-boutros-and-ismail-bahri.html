Ismaïl Bahri is artist. He once exposed at La Criée in the frame of Incorporated!, the 5th edition of Biennial of contemporary art, Rennes,2016.
Charbel-joseph H. Boutros and Ismaïl Bahri are friends and share a joint artistic sensitivity. They will share they point of view about art, taking Boutros’ exhibition as starting point.

Ismaïl Bahri

Born in 1978 in Tunis, lives and works between Paris and Tunis.
Ismaïl Bahri’s work has been presented at Jeu de Paume (Paris), L’Espace Khiasma (Lilas), Staatliche Kunsthalle (Karlsruhe), Kunst Im Tunnel (Düsseldorf), the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation (Lisbon), among others. His films have been selected at international film festivals such as TIFF (Toronto), NYFF (New york), IFFR (Rotterdam), FID (Marseille).
