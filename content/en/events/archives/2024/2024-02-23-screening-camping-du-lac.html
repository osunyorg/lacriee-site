Éléonore Saintagnan, Camping du lac, 2023 fiction, 70 min

meta_description: >- description: >- summary: >- Éléonore is driving west. She breaks down in the middle of Brittany. She rents a bungalow on a campsite overlooking a lake where, it is said, a legendary beast lives. events_categories: - "events" - "outside-the-walls" contents_reading_time: seconds: 29 text: >- less than a minute contents: - kind: heading title: >- About slug: "about" position: 1 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Forced to wander around these isolated places, she discovers their inhabitants, then the tourists who move in with the heatwave. From mobile home to mobile home, she observes the present, recalls the past and allows herself to be invaded by fiction.
