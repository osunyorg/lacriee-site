What can the forest teach us about the world and society that surrounds and shapes it? How does it transform us?

body: >-

About

Léa Muller's questions and open answers will be based on her experience as an artist and forester in the Chalouzais forest, and more particularly on the four seasons of her partnership with La Criée. Her performance will combine stories, images, objects, a calendar, drinks and appetisers concocted by her partner Amélie Taillard.
