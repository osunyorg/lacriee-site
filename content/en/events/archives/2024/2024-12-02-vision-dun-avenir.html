Vision for a future is an exhibition by Pierre Jean Giloux, presented at the Lycée Professionnel Alphonse Pellé, in partnership with La Criée centre d'art contemporain. It is part of an educational project led by the students of 3rd Prepa Métiers and echoes Biomimetic Stories, the artist's exhibition at La Criée.

Vision for a future Present Madurai, a work emblematic of the work of Pierre Jean Giloux. This film in augmented reality invites us to consider a near future, in cities marked by the climate emergency. How to reinvent the city through nature and science so that it remains habitable and liveable? Through his research and achievements, Pierre Jean Giloux is at the crossroads of documentary and science fiction and anticipates the development of new links between architecture, technology and biology.
