Mesures et démesures du temps Catherine Guesde summary: >-

A philosopher of marginal and radical music, Catherine Guesde also runs a project halfway between drone and noise music under the name Cígvë. It is between these two practices that her performance at La Criée will take place.

body: >-

About

Catherine Guesde is an associate professor in the philosophy department
at the University of Paris 8 Vincennes – Saint-Denis; her research
focuses on marginal and radical forms of popular music, such as extreme
metal and noise. She co-authored with Pauline Nadrigny "The Most
Beautiful Ugly Sound in the World": Listening to Noise (Éditions Musica
Falsa, 2018) and edited the collective work Penser avec le punk (Presses
Universitaires de France, 2022), and is a member of the editorial board
of the journal La Vie des idées. In parallel with her research, she
pursues various musical projects, written or improvised, solo (Cigvë) or
collectively, always from an experimental approach to the electric
guitar.
