body: >-

About

From this observation, I want children to adopt these forms to imagine a new architecture or landscape. Line Simon
For these workshops, Line Simon has created ink stamps featuring the
shapes of the exhibition's lights. Children can use them to first create
an "orderly" composition by assembling the shapes on the sheets until
they are completely filled. They can observe the lines, curves, and
proportions. In a second step, each child can create a landscape or
architecture using the ink stamps.

Practical informations

For 8 years old  childs with an adultfreeduring : 1 h 30
