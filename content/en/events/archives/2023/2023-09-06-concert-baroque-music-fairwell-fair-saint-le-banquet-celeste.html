About

For this evening performance, a duo of artists from Le Banquet Céleste presents a collection of songs and harpsichord pieces by emblematic 17th-century English composers, centered around Henry Lawes' poignant "Ariadne's Lament." They will perform compositions by Henry Purcell, Henry Lawes, William Lawes, John Blow, and James Hart.
