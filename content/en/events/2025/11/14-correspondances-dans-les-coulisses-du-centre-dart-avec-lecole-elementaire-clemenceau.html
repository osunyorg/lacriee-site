Throughout the 2025-2026 season, fourth and fifth graders at Clémenceau Elementary School in Rennes will participate in an immersion program featuring exhibitions and behind-the-scenes tours of the art center. 

body: >-

About

Students will have the opportunity to view images and videos of the installations at La Criée in class, meet and interview the chief stage manager, discover the season's exhibitions, and participate in a workshop at the Pasteur edulab on editing and producing podcasts. They will also get a sneak preview of the new artists' game   Rejouer La Criée .  The podcasts produced by the students will be broadcast in the 
Correspondances , digital space and during the exhibition celebrating La Criée's 40th anniversary.
