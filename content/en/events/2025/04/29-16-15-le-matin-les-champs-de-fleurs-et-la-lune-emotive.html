Exhibition showcasing the co-creations of CE2 students from Jean Moulin School and various objects series from artists Lucie Férézou, Margaux Janisset, and Gabrielle Manglou, created during the residency "La multiplicité des étoiles", from November 2024 to April 2025.

body: >-


From January to April 2025, the workshops led by Lucie Férézou and Margaux Janisset with the students resulted in the creation of posters that were displayed in several chapters within Jean Moulin School and throughout the Villejean neighborhood (at the community center, the Parent-Child Space of Andrée Chedid School, the Cercle Paul Bert, the Maison Verte, the library, the community health center, the CCAS, etc.).
The exhibition at the school will be extended to the window spaces at the Tambour of the University of Rennes 2 from April 30 to May 28.

Due to the Vigipirate plan, please confirm your attendance with the school at ecole.0352207J@ac-rennes.fr or with La Criée at la-criee@ville-rennes.fr

From January to April 2025, the workshops led by Lucie Férézou and Margaux Janisset with the students resulted in the creation of posters that were displayed in several chapters within Jean Moulin School and throughout the Villejean neighborhood (at the community center, the Parent-Child Space of Andrée Chedid School, the Cercle Paul Bert, the Maison Verte, the library, the community health center, the CCAS, etc.).
