La Petite Fabrique is a creative workshop for the whole family in the Lili, la rozell et le marimba cycle of exhibitions. Artist Line Simon offers a technique for creating images from the works.

body: >-

About

In the exhibition Dark-en-ciel, Line Simon invites you to build a diorama inspired by the stories and settings in the videos Mineur, mineur and La Brigada . A diorama is a staged landscape in a box, with one or more planes, that tells a story.
This workshop is intended for deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals. It will be translated into French Sign Language (LSF).

Practical informations

-- for children aged 6 and over, accompanied by an adult -- free of charge -- duration: 1 h 30
