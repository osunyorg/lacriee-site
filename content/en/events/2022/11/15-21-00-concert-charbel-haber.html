One day in the future, will take place the last art exhibition. Charbel-Joseph H. Boutros invites the musician Charbel Haber to compose a tribute concert for this last exhibition to come.

body: >-

A propos

The concert will be filmed, and Charbel-joseph H. Boutros will produce a
new work that will be featured in his future exhibitions. Therefore,
individuals present at the concert may be visible in the video artwork.

Practical informations

-- free admission, subject to availability -- limited number of seats
