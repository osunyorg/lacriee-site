About

Carte blanche to Fanny Vicens, accordionPianist and accordionist Fanny Vicens leads an international career that
combines her dual profile, from classical recitals to contemporary
music. Through her interactions with composers, she fosters a reflection
on the sonic identity of the accordion, which is echoed in the creation
of the microtonal accordion XAMP. The Autres Mesures festival gives her
carte blanche to reveal all its astonishing richness.Alexis Degrenier, Membres Fantômes, drumsAfter a long period away from the stage, grappling with illness, Alexis
Degrenier, a founding member of the trio La Tène, developed this
cathartic solo. He explores the multiple ramifications of the body and
its prostheses as potential resonators of an highly singular organic and tribal music.

Carte blanche to Fanny Vicens, accordion

Pianist and accordionist Fanny Vicens leads an international career that combines her dual profile, from classical recitals to contemporary music. Through her interactions with composers, she fosters a reflection on the sonic identity of the accordion, which is echoed in the creation of the microtonal accordion XAMP. The Autres Mesures festival gives her carte blanche to reveal all its astonishing richness.

Alexis Degrenier, Membres Fantômes, drums

After a long period away from the stage, grappling with illness, Alexis Degrenier, a founding member of the trio La Tène, developed this cathartic solo. He explores the multiple ramifications of the body and its prostheses as potential resonators of an highly singular organic and tribal music.
