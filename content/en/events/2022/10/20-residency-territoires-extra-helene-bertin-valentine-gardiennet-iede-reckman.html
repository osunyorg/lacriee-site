For the 6e edition of Territoires EXTRA,The art centers La Criée and Passerelle (in Brest) collaborate with three Breton Artist-Run Spaces (art spaces managed by and for artists) and supports them in several of their creation projects, transmission projects and international residencies.

About

-- Bellevue at DouarnenezThe artist Valentine Gardiennet est en résidence at Bellevue from october 26 to november 30 2022.-- Supra, artist & designer run space à Saint-Aubin-des-LandesArtist Iede Reckman is in residence at Supra from 3 to 30 October 2022. He is offering two workshops to CM1 pupils at the Saint-Joseph school in Saint-Aubin-des-Landes-- Format in Plouézec and LeipzigThe artist Hélène Bertin is in residence in the Paimpol/Plouézec region from 22 October to 21 November 2022.support:-- Ministère de la Culture / Drac Bretagne
Initiated in 2017 by the art centers Passerelle in Brest and La Criée in Rennes, Territoires EXTRA develops around three areas of work: supporting artistic emergence in Brittany through production and distribution; building an artistic project in connection with Breton territorial actors; embedding artistic emergence in an international dynamic. Territoires EXTRA takes the form of research and/or creation artist residencies and is accompanied by specific mediation work, in order to strengthen the links between audiences, local partners, institutions and artistic projects, with a focus on transmission and awareness around the presence of artists in the territories.
