On the occasion of the Sculpter (faire à l'atelier) exhibition, the European School of Art in Brittany – Rennes campus and the University of Rennes 2 – Department of Visual Arts are organising two days of reflection on sculpture in close collaboration with the Rennes Museum of Fine Arts, La Criée Centre d'art and Frac Bretagne. 

body: >-

About

In this context, bringing together the works of sixty artists since the 1980s, we wanted to invite artists and critics to address a few themes that run through the field of contemporary sculpture. -- The studio - is it still essential, how is it being reinvented? -- The links and connections between sculpture and decor, illusion and mimesis. -- What gestures and (im)materials are used in contemporary sculpture? Wednesday, 4 April 2018 -- The workshop without walls   moderated by Catherine Elkar and John Cornu 9:45 a.m. Laurent Tixador, artist 10:15 a.m. Christian Alendete, exhibition curator 11:15 a.m. Richard Leydier, art critic 11:45 a.m. Nicolas Floc'h, artist -- Changing scenery and other scenes moderated by Odile Le Borgne 2:30 p.m. Hélène Bertin, artist, Camouflaged Sculptures 3:00 p.m. Thomas Golsenne, lecturer in the history of images, Ornament and Revolution. From William Morris to Lamarche Oviz. 3:45 p.m. Lise Lerichomme, lecturer in visual arts, artist, Voluptuous, Deceptive, Impeccable. Historical Overlays in the Work of Shanon Bool, Chiara Fumaï and Lucy McKenzie

Thursday, 5 April 2018 -- Mimetic truths or fatal illusions Moderator: Christophe Viart 9:30 a.m. Virginie Yassef, artist, Anything that had feathers was a bird 10:00 a.m. Jean-Marc Huitorel, critic, exhibition curator, Towards a mimesis of distinction 10:45 a.m. Gilles Barbier, artist, Exchange with... 11:15 Corinne Charpentier, critic, exhibition curator,  Inner States -- Gestures and (im)materials in sculpture moderated by Sophie Kaplan and Nicolas Floc'h 2:30 p.m. Grégory Gicquel, artist 3:00 p.m. Ingrid Luquet-Gad, art critic 3:45 p.m. Dominique Blais, Breathing Life into Matter 4:15 p.m. Claire Kueny, art historian, Gold Leaf, Caresses and the Crackling Sounds of Yesteryear. Sculpting to Approach Time

organisation Christophe Viart, Nicolas Floc'h, Odile Le Borgne, Maëva Blandin, « Formes du temps » Unité de recherche de l'EESAB John Cornu, Yann Sérandour, Unité de recherche Pratiques et Théories de l'art contemporain EA7472, Université Rennes 2 Anne Dary, Catherine Elkar et Sophie Kaplan

informations Les Champs Libres 10 cours des Alliés, Rennes
