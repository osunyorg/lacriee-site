About

As part of the exhibition Pour ne pas dormir by Jockum Nordström at La Criée centre d'art contemporain, Melaine Dalibert offers an album of her own compositions, born from these special months. Melaine Dalibert is a pianist, composer and founding member of the contemporary music festival Autres Mesures, which La Criée has regularly hosted since 2015, with musical proposals each time imagined in response to its exhibitions
