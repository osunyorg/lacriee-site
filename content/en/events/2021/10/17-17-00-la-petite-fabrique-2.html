body: >-

About

Autumn has arrived, and the leaves are starting to fall from the trees.
Once cut, they transform into bricks, revealing their lines as they
stack up, ready to build an imaginary architecture.

Practical informations

-- for children aged 6 and over -- free -- duration: 1 h 30
