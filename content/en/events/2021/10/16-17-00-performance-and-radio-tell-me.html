body: >-

About

TELL ME 2021 focuses on the emerging scene in Brittany. Program: soap opera, stress & objects, operators, unbearable songs, magic, weightlessness, stories & drinks, dreamy and ethereal R&B, computerized languages. TELL ME is a project of the Frac Bretagne, the Musée des beaux-arts de Rennes, La Criée center for contemporary art, and 40mcube with DUUU Radio. -- Guillaume Pellay
Temps des moissons au lieu des moissons
Performance
With Sarah Blumenfeld, Thomas Delahaye, Louis Deschamps, Guillaume Pellay, Solène Robert, and Lina Schlageter
As part of his exhibition Blé at 40mcube, Guillaume Pellay presents a performance centered around physical activity and the caloric expenditure necessary for energy storage. He combines comedy, ballads, realism, rock, and dance, redeploying the sources and elements of the exhibition: his geese, Catherine Ringer and Marcia, desire, the landscape view, or heat.
Location: 40mcube
Time: 3:00 PM
Duration: 45 minutes -- Morgan Azaroff
Enseignements secrets pour changer son corps et accéder à la veille ultime Performance
With Morgan Azaroff and Camille Le Pennec
"Before, I was stressed, and I communicated this stress to the objects and forms around me, giving them a living dimension. But for the past year, I have learned to develop and control my physical and spiritual resources. I now wonder if simply looking at and feeling the environment can invent forms."
Location: Musée des beaux-arts de Rennes
Time: 4:15 PM
Duration: 20 minutes

-- Francis Raynaud
Vertigo
Performance
Francis Raynaud presents a performance featuring actress Laure Catherin. This character, an aging illusionist and magician, borrows her story from texts by Claire Guezengar to Paul Auster, via Blaise Cendrars, telling us her disappointing story of levitation.
Written by Francis Raynaud, performed by Laure Catherin
Location: Musée des beaux-arts de Rennes
Time: 5:00 PM
Duration: 20 minutes -- Anaïs Touchot
Art, Fruits secs et linge de maison
Performance "As a painter and portraitist, I have been practicing my craft in the parking lot of the Frac Bretagne for many years. Professional fairs, weddings, anniversaries, seminars, company committees, congresses, Christmas parties, municipal events, bar and bat mitzvahs, inaugurations, etc. My experience and expertise are at the service of your event's success. I also create caricatures, portraits, and silhouettes from photos. For more information: 02 99 37 37 93. Approximately 10 caricatures per hour. Student prices. The results can be spectacular but may sometimes be ineffective if met with cultural resistance."
Location: Frac Bretagne
Time: 6:00 PM – 10:00 PM
Duration: Continuous -- Emilien Chesnot
FENTANYL FLOWERS
Reading
Fentanyl flowers is an attempt to distance oneself from—and question—the computerized languages that surround us. It is an effort to see or do something with these masses of language without emitters or responses.
Location: Restaurant du Frac Bretagne
Time: 7:30 PM
Duration: 30 minutes -- Mathis Berchery
Me laisserez-vous
Performance "I adopt the figure of a storyteller at the bar, letting myself be inhabited by collected voices and numerous stories. Each story—interviews, voicemails, film scenes, poems—has its place, its glass, and its shared drink. All contribute to a geography of thirst: vital necessity, tasting pleasure, reminiscence, excess, addiction, journey."
Location: Restaurant du Frac Bretagne
Time: 8:00 PM – 10:30 PM
Duration: Continuous -- Alisson Schmitt
Cette chanson que je ne supporte pas
Performance
"There is this song I can't stand; it plays on my car radio, it plays in my mouth, so I grip the steering wheel tightly and accelerate."
Location: Frac Bretagne
Time: 9:00 PM
Duration: 20 minutes -- Rubie
The Next World
Concert
In a dream, a multi-voiced song marked by childhood and led by sound games where we seek the key to unsolved enigmas.
Location: Restaurant du Frac Bretagne
Time: 10:30 PM
Duration: 1 hour

TELL ME 2021 focuses on the emerging scene in Brittany.

Program: soap opera, stress & objects, operators, unbearable songs, magic, weightlessness, stories & drinks, dreamy and ethereal R&B, computerized languages. TELL ME is a project of the Frac Bretagne, the Musée des beaux-arts de Rennes, La Criée center for contemporary art, and 40mcube with DUUU Radio.

-- Guillaume Pellay
Temps des moissons au lieu des moissons
Performance
With Sarah Blumenfeld, Thomas Delahaye, Louis Deschamps, Guillaume Pellay, Solène Robert, and Lina Schlageter
As part of his exhibition Blé at 40mcube, Guillaume Pellay presents a performance centered around physical activity and the caloric expenditure necessary for energy storage. He combines comedy, ballads, realism, rock, and dance, redeploying the sources and elements of the exhibition: his geese, Catherine Ringer and Marcia, desire, the landscape view, or heat.
Location: 40mcube
Time: 3:00 PM
Duration: 45 minutes

-- Morgan Azaroff
Enseignements secrets pour changer son corps et accéder à la veille ultime
Performance
With Morgan Azaroff and Camille Le Pennec
"Before, I was stressed, and I communicated this stress to the objects and forms around me, giving them a living dimension.
