The family workshop is an opportunity to extend your discovery of the "Mime" exhibition by Mathis Collins and Paul Collins with an experimental workshop led by artist Line Simon.

body: >-

About

Participants will be asked to create a modular image in the form of a puzzle, revealing a Polichinelle, a mime, an artist... This imaginary character will echo the artist-policeman in Mathis Collins' works, identifiable by his headgear. The technique used will also be similar to the artist's painting process: participants will be asked to work on a wooden base and create an image by superimposing layers of color.

Practical informations

-- for children aged 6 to 12 accompanied by an adult -- free of charge -- 10 places available -- registration required: la-criee@ville-rennes.fr -- duration: 1 hour 30 minutes
