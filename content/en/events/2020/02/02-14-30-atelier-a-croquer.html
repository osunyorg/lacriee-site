Every Sunday during the Marché à manger (Food Market), La Criée contemporary art center offers a workshop for children aged 5 to 12. It's an opportunity to experiment, practice, and extend their exploration of the exhibition during a hands-on art workshop led by artist Line Simon.

body: >-


The works in Éléonore Saintagnan's exhibition will be a source of inspiration for children.

Practical informations

ages 6 to 12 ans duration 1h30 free with reservation

la-criee@ville-rennes.fr
