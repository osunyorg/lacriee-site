Echoing his residency Les appareils et les Ktronics at the Jacques Prévert school (Rennes), Jérémy Piningre presents Cardon Luneux editions with Aëla Maï Cabel.

body: >-

About

Co-founded in 2022, the micro-publishing house Cardon Lunaux brings together Jérémy Piningre and Aëla Mai Cabel. Together, they explore the rewriting of traditional tales
of the territories they pass through with contemporary concerns of gender, class and ecology.
