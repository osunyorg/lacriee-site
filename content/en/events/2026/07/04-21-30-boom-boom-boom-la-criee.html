Brave Compagnie (Johanna Rocard x Amandine Braud) summary: >-

In this collective, dance-driven synthesis of their earlier performances (Braves, La grande folie, Batailles nocturnes...), visual artist and performer Johanna Rocard and DJ Amandine Braud will serve as mistresses of ceremony for this great Saturday-night ball at the Cloître Saint-Melaine. Part ritual performance, part festive gathering, with sacred fools, witches' sabbath, charivari, carnival, krump, drag, queer, voodoo and burlesque all folded into the mix, this energetic, participatory and therapeutic dance floor works as a collective act of healing, bringing together audience members, professional dancers and local accomplices.
