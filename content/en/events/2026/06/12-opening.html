The entire La Criée team is happy to invite you to celebrate the 40thndanniversary of the art centre with the collective exhibition Bienvenue à La Criée, 40 years of creation and much more! 

The opening of this anniversary will take place on Friday, June 12th from 7pm. It will be followed by a concert evening until 10:30 pm. 

A great event weekend (bingo, meetings, etc.) will extend this opening on Saturday 13 and Sunday 14 June.

All events of this great weekend are free and open to all.
