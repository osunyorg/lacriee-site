For Bienvenue à La Criée, poet and sound artist Gilles Amalvi collects words from artists and visitors—memories of exhibitions, residencies, and encounters—which he remixes into a sound piece to be enjoyed while comfortably seated in the listening space designed for the occasion. 
To gather visitors’ memories, Gilles Amalvi sets up his mobile recording studio in front of La Criée. He will also present a live version this fall.
