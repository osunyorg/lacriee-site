La Criée and édulab Pasteur invite you to share a Magic Day!

body: >-

About

In the morning, come discover the Art is Magic exhibition by Jeremy Deller at La Criée.
In the afternoon, enjoy a poster creation workshop with graphic designer Simon Lepuissant. You can experiment with creating posters featuring repurposed slogans, inspiring yourself from Jeremy Deller's pop and quirky universe or current events. Schedule for July 21st -- 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM: Guided tour of the Art is Magic exhibition by Jeremy Deller at La Criée centre d'art contemporain
-- 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM: Free lunch in the gardens of the Palais Saint-Georges or at the Hôtel Pasteur
-- 1:30 PM: Presentation of the Édulab Pasteur and the RISO duplicator
-- 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM: Workshop with artist Simon Lepuissant at the Édulab Pasteur Additionally, other dates are available for recreation centers and social partners (July 7, 12, 13, 20, and 27, 2023).

Practical informations

-- Free event, registration required -- edulabpasteur@ville-rennes.fr -- Limited to 10 places per workshop Throughout the month of July, at La Criée and Édulab Pasteur, discover the posters created during the Magic Days with recreation centers and social partners: Prisme, the Thébaudais Psychological and Social Rehabilitation Center, the Loisirs Pluriel recreation center, and LADAPT Ouest.
