Every Sunday of the Marché à manger, La Criée centre d’art contemporain offers a workshop-visit for children. It is an opportunity to experiment, practice and extend their discovery of the exhibition during a plastic experimentation workshop led by Line Simon.

body: >-

About

The artist Line Simon invites you to discover linocut, a printing technique that allows creating an image in a manual and playful way. The works in the exhibition Personne, pas même la pluie, n'a de si petites mains  (No one, not even rain, has such small hands) will be a source of inspiration for children who can appropriate their evocative titles: 
Masque à faire tomber la neige , Les Idiots , L'histoire du lait et du miel ,
etc.

 Finally, a detail of their production will be highlighted in the gold leaf: a nod to the series of photographs by Stéphanie Saadé.

Practical informations

6-12 years duration 1h30 free of charge upon reservation la-criee@ville-rennes.fr
