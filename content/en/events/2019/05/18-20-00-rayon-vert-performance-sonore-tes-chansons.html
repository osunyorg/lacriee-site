Songs are present everywhere, at every stage of life.

body: >-

About

Listening to the relationship that each of us has with the songs, whatever their style,  Tes chansons  is a project of urban derivation according to the voices and the people the artist meets. He presents a performance in the form of a live restitution of the recorded materials.

production : La Criée centre d’art contemporain, Rennes partnership: -- PHAKT Centre culturel colombier, Rennes -- ENSAB (École Nationale Supérieure d’Architecture en Bretagne), Rennes -- radio C-Lab, Rennes -- direction de quartier centre Ville de Rennes design and realization of the cart:  Erle Marec creation of the instrument: Julien Bancilhon

Practical informations

European Night of Museums La Criée will be open until midnight duration of the performance about 40 min
