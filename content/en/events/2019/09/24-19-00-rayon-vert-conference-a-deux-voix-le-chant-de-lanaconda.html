Single Lee invites anthropologist Pierre Déléage for a two-spoke conference on common questions.

body: >-

About

It will be about the oral and written transmission of knowledge, based on their field experiences in Mexico. This meeting will also be an opportunity to question the meeting points between the research methods of the anthropologist and that of the artist, as well as their modalities of rendering.
