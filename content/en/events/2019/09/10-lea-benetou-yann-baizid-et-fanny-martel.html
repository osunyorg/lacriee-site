La Criée centre d’art contemporain has collaborated with a trio of artists from Rennes – Léa Bénétou, Yann Baïzid and Fanny Martel – to create « LiZellBa », a fun educational board game, which is also available in a digital version.
With « LiZellBa » discover the artists exhibited at the art center, enrich your knowledge, and become an apprentice curator!

body: >-

About

LiZellBa is a cooperative game that invites the public to
create a fictional exhibition. It is an amusing way for people to
discover and appropriate the works and themes that run through the
artistic cycle Lili, la rozell et le marimba (2019-2022) . The game is currently being used with mediators in the classes and
partner venues of La Criée Art Centre, and it will soon be available, at
La Criée, for everyone. It can also be played at home in a digital
version. In a game of LiZellBa , players answer questions about the
works, the exhibitions and the artists’ sources of inspiration and how
they connect with vernacular knowledge and skills. In the board version, for each correct answer, the player draws a
miniature artwork from the pot and positions it in the exhibition space
on the game board. These objects are interpretations of works produced
by La Criée. In the digital version, starting with an empty exhibition space,
players arrange images of the artworks in it as the game proceeds. Each
virtual exhibition is then photographed and takes its place in the
online collection. By playing LiZellBa, you discover the artists being shown at the art
centre, you increase your knowledge and, what’s more, you get to be an
apprentice exhibition curator.
production : -- La Criée centre d'art contemporain, Rennes

To play online

(in French) https://lizellba.org/

Associate artists

La Criée collaborates with the trio of Rennes artists Léa Bénétou, Yann
Baizid and Fanny Martel for the creation of a game of artistic education
rolling over the cycle Lili, rozell and marimba  (2019-2022).
