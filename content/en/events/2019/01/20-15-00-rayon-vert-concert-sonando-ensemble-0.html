body: >-

About

The three founding members of ensemble 0, Sylvain Chauveau, Stéphane Garin and Joël Merah propose Strongly! , a series of acoustic pieces for two guitars and metal percussion that combine in an original way the influence of minimalist repertoire and the sounds of Folk Music. This trio, after having crisscrossed Europe, Taiwan and Japan, comes to tune with the tubular bells that form the centerpiece of David Horvitz’s exhibition.
partnership : -- festival Autres mesures, Rennes -- Les Tombées de la nuit, Rennes

Practical informations

1 h free entry within the limit of available places The afternoon continues at FRAC Bretagne with four performances of Cécile Bart’s.
