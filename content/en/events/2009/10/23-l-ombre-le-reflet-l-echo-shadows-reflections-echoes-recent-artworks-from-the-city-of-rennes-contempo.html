About

The City of Rennes art collection
is not intended for full-time exhibition. It is above all the expression of a
policy of support for artists and cultural actors on the Rennes scene, and of
the desire to build a heritage that reflects today's artistic reality.

The exhibition The Shadow,
the Reflection, the Echo at La
Criée is built around a choice of recent acquisitions for a
collection now including more than 300 works of art. This is a great
opportunity to trigger debate on art and the city, the notion of the collection
whether public or private, and the artist's place in the city. Landscape is
used as a unifying theme, a first step for the public towards the discovery of
works and artists mostly from Rennes itself.
