body: >-

About

Based on the artistic program of the art centre,
this project is made for young people who want to be included in a
project of creation which solicits actively their participation. Above
all, the aim is to discover, to share, to be initiated on contemporary
art, directly in touch with works and art professionals.

Correspondances is also a multimedia edition as a blog - both
platform of exchanges and experimental space - and a print edition
designed by Marion Kueny and handed to each participant at the end of
the year.
From the artistic program of the La Criée, the project declines
different thematic entries: collection, architecture, cinema, order and
disorder,...
Each project is developed in cooperation with partners and is adapted to the age of participants.

partnerships -- centre de loisirs du centre social Ty Blosne, Rennes -- école Liberté, Rennes -- école La Poterie, Rennes -- collège Jean Monnet, Janzé -- collège Amand Brionne, Saint-Aubin d’Aubigné -- lycée agricole Théodore Monod, Le Rheu edition : The publication, created by graphic artist Marion Kueny, brings together some of the different productions and creative approaches (drawings, photomontage, video, three-dimensional work, collages, etc.). The publication is inspired by the groups' correspondence notebooks and invites us to make posters with the covers, cutting out the pieces like a jigsaw puzzle.
