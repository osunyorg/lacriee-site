Le Banquet Céleste est un ensemble en résidence à l’Opéra de Rennes. Lucile Boulanger, Andréas Linos et Thomas Pierrefe proposent un trio de violes.

body: >-

About

From their heyday in England in the mid-17th century to the Mozart era
of the late 18th century, this trio of viols will take you on a journey
through time and across countries. The musical versatility of this
family of instruments, presented here as a trio, allows the listener to
experience the richness of various musical compositions.

Practical informations

-- 30-minute concerts-- as part of the Rennes in the Roaring Twenties festival-- booking recommended for the concert alone banquet-celeste.fr -- free guided tour accompanied by a 30-minute baroque music discovery concert Destination Rennes
